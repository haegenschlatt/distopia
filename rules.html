distopia - global rules

These rules apply to all boards.

  1. Do not post or link to content that is illegal.
  2. Do not post anyone's private/personal information.
  3. If your link goes to a page containing obscene or adult content, include some sort of warning (simply writing "NSFW" should be fine). "Shock" images and other disgusting or grotesque material is forbidden.
  4. Participating in or creating threads centered on "raiding" or causing harm/significant inconvenience to any person, party or website is prohibited.
  5. Do not post racist, homophobic, lewd, or otherwise insensitive or highly offensive content.
  6. Do not post spam. Automated posting/fetching (e.g. from a bot, script, or scraper) is forbidden. (If you would like an API, post your desires in the [meta] board.)
  7. Impersonating an Admin or mod is forbidden.
  8. Avoid posting inane comments such as "lol" or "gtfo". If you agree with a statement, do not reply with only "This". Contribute your reasoning as well.
  9. We reserve the right to ban anyone or remove any post at any time for any reason.
  10. We may move your thread to a board where it better fits the topic.
  11. Attempting to circumvent the ban, mute, or administration systems, or otherwise "hacking" the board is strictly forbidden.

[~] - random

  1. Anything goes. Threads will never be moved off this board for being off-topic.
  2. The global rules still apply, and are more strictly enforced on this board.

[meta] - meta-discussion

  1. This board is intended for discussion regarding the site. The admins will read over this board frequently.
  2. If you have complaints, bug reports, suggestions, recommendations, or questions, they should go here.
  3. Please write your message or complaint in a reasonable and comprehensible manner. We will ignore indiscernable posts.
  4. Admins will always post under the red "Admin" name. Anyone else claiming to be an administrator is an imposter.
